Frequently Asked Questions

What is AgriMentor AI?

AgriMentor AI is an AI-powered platform that provides personalized crop advice, career guidance, and market insights to farmers.

How does the AI generate advice?

Currently, the advice is generated using a mock AI model. Future versions will integrate Google Cloud's Vertex AI for real-time, personalized recommendations.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes, all user queries and data are stored securely in Firebase Firestore with proper authentication and access controls.
