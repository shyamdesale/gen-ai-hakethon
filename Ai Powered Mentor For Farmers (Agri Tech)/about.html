About AgriMentor AI

AgriMentor AI is an innovative platform built with Google Cloud, Firebase, and Vertex AI to empower farmers with personalized crop advice, career guidance, and market insights.

Our AI-driven system helps farmers optimize farming practices, manage challenges like pests and water issues, and explore career opportunities including government schemes and training programs.

The platform is designed to be farmer-friendly, mobile-optimized, and accessible, ensuring that even users with limited technical skills can benefit from advanced AI technology.
